Overview

- Moon Meet is an instant messaging application that gives you a good acces to connect with friends, make relationships, share stories etc..

- Moon Meet is made to make chatting & meeting people easier by respecting their privacy.

- Moon Meet is a new platform and it is not a remake or a re-design for another platform.
